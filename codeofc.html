Code of Conduct

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

1. Our Commitment

BLAME! is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful community for all players. We believe that a positive gaming environment benefits everyone. This Code of Conduct outlines the expectations we have for all community members.

2. Be Respectful

Treat all players, moderators, and community members with respect and dignity.

  • No harassment, bullying, or discrimination based on any characteristic
  • No hate speech, slurs, or derogatory language
  • Listen to others and engage in constructive dialogue

3. Fair Play

Play with integrity and honesty.

  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics
  • No account sharing or unauthorized access
  • Report bugs and glitches to our support team

4. Community Safety

Help us maintain a safe community.

  • No sharing of personal information without consent
  • No spam, phishing, or malicious links
  • Report suspicious activity to moderators

5. Appropriate Content

Keep all communication appropriate and suitable for a diverse audience.

  • No explicit, adult, or sexual content
  • No graphic violence or disturbing imagery
  • No political or religious extremism

6. Respect Intellectual Property

Do not infringe on copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights of BLAME! or others. Do not distribute pirated content or engage in illegal activities.

7. Follow Moderator Decisions

Respect and cooperate with BLAME! moderators and support staff. Do not argue with moderators in public channels. If you disagree with a decision, contact support privately.

8. Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

  • Warning or temporary mute
  • Temporary suspension from the game
  • Permanent ban from the community
  • Legal action if applicable

9. Report Issues

If you witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it immediately to our moderation team. Provide detailed information and evidence when possible to help us investigate.
